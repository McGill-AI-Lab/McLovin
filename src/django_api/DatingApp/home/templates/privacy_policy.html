Privacy Policy

At McGill Dating App, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This privacy policy aims to inform you how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and preferences provided during account registration.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our app, including interactions and session lengths.
  • Location Information: Optional geolocation data to improve matching accuracy.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to improve your experience on our platform:

  • Matching you with compatible profiles.
  • Sending notifications about matches or updates.
  • Providing customer support and troubleshooting issues.

3. Data Security

We use advanced encryption and industry-standard practices to protect your data from unauthorized access. Your privacy is a top priority for us.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to review, update, or delete your personal data at any time by accessing your account settings. For further assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out to us at support@mcgilldatingapp.com.
